## 💧 Drainage NOC Management System (Jal & Mal)

> ⚡ Digitizing drainage approval workflows with scalable, transparent, and efficient system design.

### 📌 Overview

The **Drainage NOC Management System** is a government-based web application developed to digitize the approval process for **Drainage Jal (Water) and Drainage Mal (Sewage) NOC services**.

It transforms the traditional offline process into a **fully online workflow**, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and faster approvals for construction-related permissions.


### 🚀 Key Features

* 📄 Online NOC Application (CC & OC)
* 🔁 Multi-Level Approval Workflow
* 💳 Online Payment & Demand Generation
* 📊 Service-wise Dashboard & Reports
* 🔗 API-Based Data Autofetch (BPMS)
* 📑 Receipt Generation
* 🔍 Real-Time Application Tracking
* 📥 Final NOC Upload System

---

### 🏗️ Modules

#### 🔹 Commencement Certificate (CC)

* Application Submission by Architect
* Site Inspection by Authority
* Demand Generation & Payment
* Final Approval & NOC Upload

#### 🔹 Occupancy Certificate (OC)

* Auto-fetch of CC Project Data
* Multi-Level Authority Review
* Payment & Receipt Generation
* Final NOC Approval

---

### ⚙️ Workflow

1. Login to Portal
2. Select ULB & Project
3. Choose Drainage NOC Service
4. Fill Application Details
5. Submit Application
6. Authority Inspection & Verification
7. Demand Generation & Payment
8. Receipt Generation
9. Final Review & NOC Upload
10. Process Completion

---
